Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Data Collection

AutoMail Day Planner accesses your Gmail data solely to provide email organization and planning features. We collect:

  • Email metadata (subject, sender, date, category)
  • Email content for analysis and display
  • OAuth access tokens for authentication

2. Data Storage

All data is processed and stored locally in your browser. We do NOT:

  • Store your emails on external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell or monetize your information
  • Track your activity beyond the app

3. Data Security

Your data is protected through:

  • OAuth 2.0 authentication via Google
  • Client-side encryption and processing
  • No server-side data storage
  • Automatic session cleanup on logout

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access at any time via Google Account settings
  • Clear all locally stored data
  • Request information about data usage

5. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us via GitHub: @dhilip-s-e
